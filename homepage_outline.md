# BuilderAscent Homepage Outline

## 1. Tone and Message Direction
*   **Tone:** Direct, conversational, and no-nonsense. It should feel like advice from a respected foreman or a trusted mentor on the job site.
*   **Vocabulary:** Avoid corporate jargon (like "synergy" or "paradigm shift") and academic language. Use grounded, relatable terms (tools, foundation, blueprint, hard work, leveling up).
*   **Message:** Hard work gets you in the door, but the right skills and habits help you run the job. We provide the practical tools you need to stop grinding in the same spot and start moving up.

## 2. Hero Section (Top of the Page)
*This is the first thing they see. It needs to clearly state what you do and who it's for.*

*   **Main Headline:** The Blueprint for Your Construction Career.
*   **Short Sub-Headline:** 
   You’re already working hard. BuilderAscent gives you the tools, habits, and direction to actually move forward in your construction career.
*   **Primary Call to Action (CTA) Button:** "Start Building Your Future", "Get Your Blueprint"

## 3. Homepage Sections (Scrolling Down)

### Section 1: The Problem (Why they are here)
*   **Heading:** You’re Working Hard… But Not Getting Ahead
*   **Content:** Long days. Early mornings. Physical work.

But nobody’s showing you how to actually move up.

You can grind for years and still feel stuck in the same spot—same pay, same role, same frustration.

Hard work isn’t the problem. Direction is.

### Section 2: The Solution (How BuilderAscent helps)
*   **Heading:** "Give Yourself the Right Tools for the Job."
*   **Content:** BuilderAscent is built for people in the trades who want more—but don’t have a clear path to get there.

We don’t give you fluff or motivation speeches. We give you real tools you can use on the job.

*   **Build Real Skills:** Learn what actually gets noticed—leadership, communication, and jobsite awareness
*   **Build Better Habits:** Show up prepared, stay organized, and separate yourself from the average worker
*   **Build Your Career:** Follow a clear path from laborer → skilled worker → foreman → beyond

This is the blueprint for moving up—without guessing your way through it.

### Section 3: How It Works (Keep it dead simple)
*   **Heading:** "How to Level Up"
*   **Content:** A simple system you can actually follow:

1.  **Join the Crew:** Sign up and get access to the tools and training
2.  **Learn the Blueprint:** Get real-world strategies you can apply immediately
3.  **Apply It on the Job:** Use what you learn to stand out, earn more, and move up
