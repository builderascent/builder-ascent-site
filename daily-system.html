The Daily Builder System

Morning Plan — 5 Minutes

  • What are we doing today?
  • What could slow us down?
  • What materials, tools, or information do we need?
  • Who needs to know the plan?

Midday Check — 2 Minutes

  • Are we on track?
  • What is slowing us down?
  • What needs to be fixed now?
  • Who needs an update?

End of Day Review — 5 Minutes

  • What got done?
  • What did not get done?
  • What caused delays or waste?
  • What needs to be ready for tomorrow?

Builder Ascent Rules

If you’re reacting, you’re already behind.
If your crew is waiting, something was missed.
If you don’t track it, you don’t control it.
If you bring problems without solutions, you are not leading yet.
If you prepare tomorrow today, you separate yourself.

Field Scenarios

Scenario 1

Crew is standing around because material is missing.

Most People

Wait, blame, or complain.

Builder Ascent Move

Shift to productive work, notify leadership early, confirm delivery status, and prepare the next task.

Scenario 2

Another trade is blocking your work area.

Most People

Get frustrated and stop.

Builder Ascent Move

Communicate early, coordinate access, adjust sequence, and keep the crew productive.

Scenario 3

The plan changes halfway through the day.

Most People

Get confused or waste time.

Builder Ascent Move Reset the crew, update priorities, identify new risks, and document the change.
